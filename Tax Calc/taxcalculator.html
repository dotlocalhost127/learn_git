Salary Tax Slabs According to Budget 2022-23

Salary income exceeds PKR 6 Million but is under PKR 12 Million
If the annual salary income does not exceed PKR. 600,000 then the rate of the income tax is 0%
Salary income exceeds PKR 600,000 but is under PKR 1.2 Million
If the annual salary income does not exceed PKR. 600,000 then the rate of the income tax is 0%
Salary income exceeds PKR 1.2 Million but is under PKR 2.4 Million

Salary income exceeds PKR 2.4 Million but is under PKR 3.6 Million
If the annual salary income does not exceed PKR. 600,000 then the rate of the income tax is 0%
